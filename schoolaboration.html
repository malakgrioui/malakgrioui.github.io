Schoolaboration

A volunteer management platform for students.

Company

La Cordée – a school dedicated to providing equal opportunities through innovative education for underprivileged children, supported by Espérance Banlieues and HOPE.

Context

The project sought to simplify volunteer scheduling, improve engagement, and optimize administrative processes through an intuitive digital platform, reducing time spent on management and increasing accessibility for both students and educators.

Key Contributions

  • Tool Implementation & System Overhaul: Integrated a new volunteer management tool, replacing the existing inefficient system to improve usability and coordination.
  • Database Restructuring: Designed and implemented a more efficient database architecture to enhance data organization, retrieval, and reporting.
  • System Analysis & Optimization: Conducted an in-depth evaluation of the existing tool, identifying pain points and proposing strategic enhancements to streamline volunteer management.
  • Stakeholder Coordination: Worked closely with La Cordée coordinators, volunteers, and external partners to align project goals and ensure seamless adoption.
  • User Research & Feedback Collection: Organized brainstorming sessions, conducted user story mapping, and gathered feedback to refine platform functionalities.
  • Process Optimization & Documentation: Developed comprehensive guides and documentation to help coordinators adapt to the new system, reducing onboarding time and ensuring smooth transitions.

Methodology & Tools

  • Approach: Agile (Scrum) – Iterative development cycles and continuous user feedback integration.

  • Tools Used:
    • Project Management: Miro
    • Collaboration: Microsoft Workspace

Lessons Learned

  • System analysis is crucial for effective transformation, ensuring inefficiencies are properly identified and addressed.
  • Database optimization significantly enhances performance, reducing operational bottlenecks and improving data accessibility.
  • Continuous feedback drives improvement, refining platform usability and engagement over time.
