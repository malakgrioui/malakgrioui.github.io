La Cordée
Non-profit Digital Transformation Volunteer Management

Schoolaboration

A volunteer management platform that digitized and streamlined coordination for a school dedicated to equal opportunity education.

Company

La Cordée is a school dedicated to providing equal opportunities through innovative education for underprivileged children, supported by Espérance Banlieues and HOPE. Their mission relies heavily on volunteer engagement — making operational efficiency a direct enabler of educational impact.

Context & Challenge

Managing a large volunteer base manually was creating bottlenecks that limited La Cordée's reach and impact. The project sought to simplify scheduling, improve engagement, and optimize administrative processes through an intuitive digital platform — reducing coordination overhead and increasing accessibility for students and educators alike.

Key Contributions

  • Tool Implementation & System Overhaul Integrated a new volunteer management tool to replace the inefficient legacy system.
  • Database Restructuring Designed a more efficient database architecture to improve data organization, retrieval, and reporting.
  • System Analysis & Optimization Evaluated the existing tools in depth, identifying pain points and proposing strategic enhancements.
  • Stakeholder Coordination Worked with coordinators, volunteers, and external partners to ensure smooth adoption and alignment.
  • User Research & Feedback Organized brainstorming sessions, conducted user story mapping, and gathered feedback to refine functionalities.
  • Process Documentation Developed comprehensive guides to reduce onboarding time and ensure sustainable transitions.

Methodology & Tools

  • Approach Agile (Scrum) — iterative development with continuous user feedback integration.
  • Project Management Miro
  • Collaboration Microsoft Workspace

Lessons Learned

  • System analysis is the prerequisite of effective transformation — without deeply understanding existing inefficiencies, solutions risk solving the wrong problem.
  • Database optimization has compounding effects: cleaner data structures reduce operational friction long after the project ends.
  • In non-profit contexts, adoption hinges on trust and simplicity — feature richness matters far less than intuitive, low-friction workflows.
