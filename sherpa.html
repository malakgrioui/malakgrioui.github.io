Start-Up Sherpa

An AI-powered platform democratizing entrepreneurial support.

Company

In collaboration with The Sustainable Grid (TSG), a cutting-edge initiative providing smart energy and digital infrastructure solutions to drive sustainable business growth and innovation.

Context

The project aimed to address the high failure rate of startups by providing accessible resources, personalized diagnostics, and expert connections to help entrepreneurs refine and scale their business ideas.

Key Contributions

  • Product Roadmap & Strategy: Developed a structured roadmap aligned with startup needs and market trends.
  • User Experience & Engagement: Conducted research to understand user pain points and refine platform features.
  • AI & Automation: Defined AI-driven diagnostics and automated business analysis reports to assist entrepreneurs.
  • UX/UI Design: Created platform wireframes and interactive prototypes to enhance user experience and interface intuitiveness.

Methodology & Tools

  • Approach: Agile (Scrum) – Implemented iterative sprints and backlog refinement.

  • Tools Used:
    • Project Management: Miro
    • Collaboration: Microsoft Workspace
    • Data & AI: OpenAI
    • UX/UI: Figma

Lessons Learned

  • AI-driven tools can significantly enhance business analysis, providing fast and actionable insights for entrepreneurs.
  • Building a strong network of mentors and investors is key to driving user engagement and long-term platform success.
  • Cross-functional collaboration accelerates innovation, ensuring technical feasibility aligns with business strategy.
