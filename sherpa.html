Start-Up Sherpa

An AI-powered platform democratizing entrepreneurial support — diagnostics, expert guidance, and networking for early-stage founders.

Company

Built in collaboration with The Sustainable Grid (TSG), a cutting-edge initiative providing smart energy and digital infrastructure solutions to drive sustainable business growth and innovation.

Context & Challenge

The majority of early-stage startups fail not from lack of ambition, but from lack of access to the right guidance at the right time. Start-Up Sherpa was conceived to address this gap — providing accessible resources, personalized diagnostics, and expert connections to help entrepreneurs refine and scale their ideas before they run out of runway.

Key Contributions

  • Product Roadmap & Strategy Developed a structured roadmap aligned with startup needs, market trends, and platform constraints.
  • User Experience & Engagement Conducted research to understand founder pain points and shaped features around real workflows.
  • AI & Automation Design Defined AI-driven diagnostics and automated business analysis reports to assist entrepreneurs at scale.
  • UX/UI Design Created platform wireframes and interactive prototypes to optimize user experience and interface clarity.

Methodology & Tools

  • Approach Agile (Scrum) — iterative sprints and backlog refinement.
  • UX/UI Design Figma
  • AI Integration OpenAI API
  • Project Management Miro
  • Collaboration Microsoft Workspace

Lessons Learned

  • AI-driven tools can dramatically accelerate business diagnostics — but only if the underlying data models reflect real founder challenges, not theoretical ones.
  • Building a mentor and investor network early is a growth multiplier for platform engagement and trust.
  • Cross-functional collaboration accelerates innovation — technical feasibility and business strategy must be co-developed, not handed off sequentially.
