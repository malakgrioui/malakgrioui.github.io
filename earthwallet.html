EarthWallet

A B2B2C platform enabling banks to integrate sustainability into their financial products.

Company

Handprint.Tech – A tech startup focused on embedding sustainability into everyday transactions.

Context

The goal was to develop a scalable financial product allowing banks to offer eco-friendly payment cards, empowering millions of consumers to fund environmental restoration projects with every transaction. The project required a robust product strategy, seamless UX, and a strong technical foundation for global scalability.

Key Contributions

  • Product Roadmap: Defined and implemented a structured roadmap aligning with business goals and market needs.
  • User & Market Research: Conducted competitive analysis and user interviews to define product-market fit.
  • Functional Specifications: Created detailed user stories, requirement specifications, and system diagrams to guide development.
  • Cross-functional Coordination: Managed collaboration between engineering, design, and business teams to ensure timely and high-quality delivery.
  • API & Data Architecture: Contributed to defining data architecture and API integrations for seamless transactions.
  • Stakeholder Communication: Worked closely with banking partners and sustainability organizations to align on product features.
  • Testing & QA: Oversaw product testing phases, ensuring high standards for usability and performance.

Methodology & Tools

  • Approach: Agile (Scrum) – Implemented iterative sprints with continuous user feedback and adjustments.

  • Tools Used:
    • Project Management: Jira, Azure
    • Design & Prototyping: Figma, Miro
    • Development & API Management: Postman
    • Collaboration: Slack, Notion, Google Workspace

Lessons Learned

  • Aligning tech with sustainability goals requires deep collaboration between financial institutions, regulators, and tech teams.
  • Iterative development & rapid feedback loops significantly enhance product-market fit, allowing quick pivots when necessary.
  • Cross-functional communication is key, bridging gaps between engineering, design, and business units ensures seamless execution.
