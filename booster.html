Videoportrait
Business Strategy Digital Transformation SME Growth Branding

Booster SME

Digital transformation and business growth strategy for a local video production company — from brand identity to sales optimization and digital tooling.

Company

Videoportrait is a company specializing in professional video solutions. As a growing SME, it needed a structured approach to modernize its brand, expand its market reach, and build sustainable operational processes.

Context & Challenge

The project aimed to expand Videoportrait's market reach by refining sales strategies, strengthening brand identity, implementing a structured communication plan, and optimizing digital tools — all to drive long-term customer growth, engagement, and operational scalability from a lean starting point.

Key Contributions

  • Business Development & Sales Strategy Designed and implemented a sales growth plan to strengthen Videoportrait's local market presence.
  • Brand Identity Developed a cohesive brand identity — aligning visual elements and messaging to improve market differentiation.
  • Strategic Communication Created a structured communication strategy to improve customer outreach and engagement quality.
  • Website & UX Optimization Designed an intuitive, conversion-focused website to improve customer retention and digital first impressions.
  • Project Management Oversaw cross-functional alignment between sales, marketing, and development efforts toward shared goals.

Methodology & Tools

  • Approach Agile (Scrum) — iterative cycles with continuous feedback integration.
  • Project Management Miro, Trello
  • Design & Branding Figma, Canva
  • Collaboration Google Workspace

Lessons Learned

  • Strategic sales planning is most effective when grounded in specific customer segments — broad strategies dilute effort and impact.
  • A compelling, consistent brand identity builds customer trust faster than any individual marketing campaign.
  • For SMEs, digital transformation succeeds when tools are selected for adoption ease, not feature count — simplicity is a competitive advantage.
