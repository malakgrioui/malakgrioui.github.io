Booster SME

Business Development & Digital Strategy

Company

Videoportrait – A company specializing in professional video solutions.

Context

The project aimed to expand Videoportrait’s market reach by refining sales strategies, strengthening brand identity, implementing a structured communication plan, and optimizing digital tools to drive long-term growth, customer engagement, and operational scalability.

Key Contributions

  • Business Development & Sales Strategy: Designed and implemented a sales growth plan to strengthen Videoportrait’s presence in the local market.
  • Brand Identity Creation: Developed a fresh and cohesive brand identity, aligning visual and messaging elements to enhance market differentiation.
  • Strategic Communication Planning: Created and executed a structured communication strategy to improve customer outreach and engagement.
  • Website Development & UX Optimization: Designed an intuitive, conversion-driven website improving customer experience and retention.
  • Project Management: Oversaw cross-functional collaboration to align sales, marketing, and development efforts with company goals.

Methodology & Tools

  • Approach: Agile (Scrum) – Iterative development cycles and continuous user feedback integration.

  • Tools Used:
    • Project Management: Miro, Trello
    • Design & Branding: Figma, Canva
    • Collaboration & Documentation: Google Workspace

Lessons Learned

  • Strategic sales planning drives business expansion, ensuring sustainable market penetration.
  • A compelling brand identity enhances customer trust, improving competitive positioning.
  • Effective communication plans improve engagement, enabling stronger connections with target audiences.
