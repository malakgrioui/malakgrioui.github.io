Dositrace

Next-Generation Dose Management Software for Medical Imaging.

Company

Biomediqa – A leader in Radiation Protection, Medical Physics, and Internal Quality Control, providing innovative solutions for medical imaging, research, and compliance to ensure patient safety and operational excellence.

Context

The project focused on a comprehensive upgrade of Dositrace, enhancing its UX/UI for a more intuitive experience, streamlining workflow automation to reduce manual tasks, and integrating advanced data interoperability for seamless communication between medical imaging devices and hospital systems.

Key Contributions

  • Product Roadmap & Strategy: Defined and executed the roadmap for Dositrace 4.0, incorporating user feedback and compliance updates to enhance functionality.
  • Cross-functional Team Coordination: Managed collaboration between developers, healthcare professionals, and quality assurance teams.
  • UX/UI Design: Created user-friendly wireframes and interactive prototypes to enhance interface intuitiveness.
  • Process Optimization: Redesigned user workflows, improving efficiency in ionizing dose management.
  • Medical Device Integration: Ensured seamless connection of Dositrace with over 20 medical imaging devices.

Methodology & Tools

  • Approach: Agile (Scrum) – Implemented iterative sprints and backlog refinement.

  • Tools Used:
    • Project Management: Miro
    • Collaboration: Microsoft Workspace
    • Medical Data & Compliance: Linux, SQL, HL7 Integration
    • UX/UI: Figma

Lessons Learned

  • UX/UI improvements drive software adoption, making usability a key priority in medical applications.
  • Interdisciplinary collaboration is crucial, ensuring alignment between healthcare professionals and software developers.
  • Medical data integration requires rigorous validation, ensuring seamless and secure interoperability.
